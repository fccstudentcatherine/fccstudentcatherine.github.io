You Should Know ...a little more of the basics.

There are some things that you should just know. As our society becomes more technologically advanced it is in our best interest to understand what it is we are interacting with and relying on. This section is meant to highlight some of the topics covered in CIT82 that I found interesting and useful to anyone, not just web developers.

 

I found it very informative to know about all the elements connected to my network. Knowing how everything is connected and making an educated decision about how it is all configured has been enlightening. Learning about the speed of my devices and the semantics of versions was fun and revealing. I learned that the internet connection on my phone is extremely slow at best. I also learned how to tell a significant update from a minor one. Knowing whether I am on a web app or a web site may not be life altering but I found it very interesting. It is the small bits of information like this that we pass by every day that, once they are explained, can change your perspective on things.
