(Lecture notes from lynda.com) Understanding Search Engine Optimization What is SEO for ecommerce The process of assessing the current on and off page factors of a site Improving these factors to boost ranking in a search engine like Google Optimization - making fully functional Traffic and ranking correlation Understanding on-page vs. off-page SEO On page: Changes you can make directly on the site, Keywords, content, urls Off page: links from other sites or social media, blog posts or product review The importance of usability Easy to use for customers Acquisition and retention Design and write for customers first and SE second Make info easy to find Focus on proper design techniques Exploring how SEO affects the buying process Need recognition, solution exploration, evaluate options, buy, evaluate Ex. insole sales target “pain while running” Target people who are looking at reviews Setting expectations and establishing realistic goals for your SEO results Results shift quickly up and down Ensure your site deserves to be ranked top Target specific searches Google grades your site Pay your way to the top initially
