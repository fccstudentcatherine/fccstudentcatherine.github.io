Web Development Opportunities ...it is the future.

At the end of the day we are going to school to acquire new skills that make us more marketable. If we are lucky we find an area of study that is intriguing and that we find a passion for. While this is not my passion, it is very interesting. This is the type of subject that should be required for everyone to know because it is simply part of all of our lives. It is near impossible to exist in our modern society without interacting with technology in some way.

 

Knowing what opportunities are available and what functionalities each job is responsible for is important. We find respect in knowledge. I respect those who make my life easier by implementing apps and websites that save me precious time and often money. There are people that are naturally technologically savvy and there are opportunities for those people to find a specialty or to become experts at everything.

UX Developer

It is all about the experience.

UI Developer

It is all about how it works.

Front End Designer

Their specialty is what we see.

Back End Designer

Their specialty is what we do not see.

Full Stack Designer

Their specialty is a little bit of everything.
