UI Developer

(Week 10, no content for specific UI Dev)I found this interesting article from a UX developer. It is a little old; it was written in 2011 but it does a good job of telling the reader what a UX developer is and what he/she should expect as part of a web design and development team. An interesting point made in the article is that the UX developer wouldn’t build something just to see if it “works”. Of course that would be important but their job is more to see if a particular functionality is going to fulfill a goal such as driving traffic to a specific area of the site. Their focus may be on whether the element is intuitive and if it flows easily. The experience of a UX developer seems to come more from front end design rather than back end. It is important that they have a thorough understanding from start to finish because often the UX developer is the bridge between the designers and the traditional developers. They need to be able to explain why something didn’t work or why a particular feature needs to be changed to make the user’s experience better.
